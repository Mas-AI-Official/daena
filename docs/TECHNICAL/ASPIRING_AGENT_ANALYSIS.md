## üéØ YOUR IDEA: "Take Over All Agents in the World"

### 5 Questions You Haven't Thought About:

1. **Market Entry Strategy**
   - **Question**: How do you compete with OpenAI's GPTs, Anthropic's Agents, and Microsoft's Copilot Studio when they have billions in funding and established ecosystems?
   - **Why it matters**: You can't just build better tech - you need a defensible moat and go-to-market strategy.

   - **Question**: What happens when governments regulate AI agents? Do you have GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2 compliance built-in from day 1?
   - **Why it matters**: Enterprise customers won't buy without compliance. This could kill your product before it starts.

3. **Network Effects & Lock-In**
   - **Question**: How do you create switching costs? Why would customers choose Daena over existing solutions that integrate with their current tools?
   - **Why it matters**: Without lock-in, customers can easily switch. You need sticky features.

4. **Scaling Economics**
   - **Question**: At what scale does Daena become profitable? What's your unit economics? How much does each agent cost to run?
   - **Why it matters**: You might have the best tech, but if it costs $100 to serve a $10 customer, you'll fail.

5. **Intellectual Property Protection**
   - **Question**: What if OpenAI/Anthropic copy your NBMF format or council system? Do you have patents filed? Can you defend them?
   - **Why it matters**: Big tech can out-execute you. You need legal protection.
