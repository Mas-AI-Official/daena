Here’s how I run the loop. Every request comes through Intake — goals, constraints, or tasks. The CMP Router directs it to the right agents, who execute within their departments. Each step is captured in shared and departmental memory, with governance built in to keep everything aligned. Finally, we deliver Goal-Aligned Output in real time. The loop never stops: intake → routing → execution → memory & governance → output — and back again, continuously improving.
